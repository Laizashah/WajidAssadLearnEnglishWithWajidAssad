Meet Our CEO

Wajid Assad is the visionary CEO of the website Learn English with Wajid Assad. Armed with an impressive academic portfolio that includes a Master’s degree in English, a Bachelor’s degree in English Literature, and a Diploma in Applied Linguistics, he has devoted over seven years to empowering students with transformative language skills. His expertise lies in accent refinement, grammar mastery, vocabulary enhancement, public speaking, and developing overall English fluency. Wajid's passion for language education is evident in his commitment to delivering practical, results-driven learning experiences. Wajid is joined by his brother, Sajid Assad, who brings equally commendable qualifications and experience to the team. Sajid holds a Postgraduate degree in English and collaborates with Wajid to provide a holistic approach to language training. Together, they create a dynamic learning environment that fosters confidence, effective communication, and measurable progress. Their combined dedication, extensive experience, and academic excellence have made them a trusted name in language education, helping countless students achieve their linguistic and professional aspirations..
